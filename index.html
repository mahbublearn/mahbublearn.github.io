How long before I see results?
SEO results typically show improvement within 4–8 weeks. YouTube Ads show results much faster — often within the first week. Full channel growth compounds over 3–6 months with consistent execution.
Do you work with new channels or only established ones?
Both. The Channel Audit package is specifically designed for brand-new or early-stage channels. The Standard Growth and Full Channel Growth System plans work best for channels that already have some content and are ready to scale.
What niches do you specialize in?
I've worked across many niches — tech, finance, education, e-commerce, lifestyle, and personal brands. My strategies are data-driven and adaptable to any niche, not one-size-fits-all.
Can I cancel my monthly plan anytime?
Yes. The Growth plan is a monthly retainer and you can cancel at any time. I believe in results-driven relationships — you should stay because you're seeing growth, not because you're locked in.

I accept Western Union (recommended), PayPal, Payoneer, Skrill, and Cash App. For Upwork clients, payments are handled securely through the Upwork platform.
